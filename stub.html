Features

Combat

Battle through various areas and dungeons, obtaining valuable items as you progress...

  • Battling: Fight monsters, collecting dungeon keys and components to increase your power...
  • Dungeoneering: Make your way through dungeons to obtain loot from the chest at the end...

Gathering

Gather resources to help build up a clan, gathering valuable items required for crafting along the way.

  • Mining: Gather metal for your clan, and collect gems to craft rings...
  • Woodcutting: Gather wood for your clan, collecting sap to craft potions...
  • Quarrying: Gather stone for your clan, collecting valuable stones to craft runes...

Crafting

All items are crafted by players. Only the highest level crafters can make the highest level items. Choose your crafting skills and supply the items on the market.

  • Runecrafting: Craft runes to provide benefits in battlings...
  • Jewelcrafting: Craft rings to increase your power...
  • Alchemy: Craft potions that boost resources, experience and more...

Clans

Create or join a clan to team up with other players.

  • Battlegrounds: Defeat bosses and mobs in your clan battlegrounds to level up your clan...
  • Buildings: Use your resources to create buildings to provide benefits to your clan, and all members...
